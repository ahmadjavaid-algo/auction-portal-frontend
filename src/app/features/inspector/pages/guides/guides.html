General Inspection Principles

Foundation

Follow a consistent, structured inspection flow to ensure accuracy, safety, and objectivity. Always document issues clearly and capture supporting photos.

  • check_circle
    Maintain a neutral, unbiased inspection mindset
  • check_circle
    Follow the predefined inspection order
  • check_circle
    Verify VIN/chassis before starting
  • check_circle
    Capture clear photos of all required angles

Exterior Checklist

Essential

Inspect all outer areas of the vehicle with proper angles and lighting conditions.

  • check_circle
    Body panels: dents, scratches, paint mismatch
  • check_circle
    Glass: cracks, chips, tint damage
  • check_circle
    Tires: tread depth, uneven wear patterns
  • check_circle
    Lights: headlamps, indicators, brake lights
event_seat

Interior Assessment

Critical

Thoroughly evaluate the cabin for usability, wear, and operational correctness.

  • check_circle
    Seat condition, stains, tears, adjustments
  • check_circle
    Dashboard indicators functioning properly
  • check_circle
    AC/heater performance and controls
  • check_circle
    Odor or leakage signs inside cabin

Engine & Mechanical

Technical

Ensure the vehicle's mechanical components are performing correctly and safely.

  • check_circle
    Engine noise, vibration, starting behavior
  • check_circle
    Oil, coolant levels and visible leaks
  • check_circle
    Belts, hoses, radiator condition
  • check_circle
    Exhaust smoke color and emissions
